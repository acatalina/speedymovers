Who we are

Speedy Movers are a family run team offering removals, storage and courier services. We are based in Hindley & Leigh and offer our services throughout the north west and nationwide We are a small family run business based in Hindley and Leigh.

We offer removals, self store and nationwide courier services. We take on all jobs no matter how big or small. Whether it be domestic or commercial. We have been in business for over 5 years and pride ourselves on our affordability, service, and going the extra mile for our customers. Literally! We have done relocations to Spain and the Scottish Highlands. We offer a packing and wrapping service and we also can take down and reassemble flat pack furniture. Our removals team are friendly and professional and nothing is too much trouble, Our storage facilities are suitable for all your needs. Whether you're moving house and need some temporary storage, to storing camping equipment, Furniture storage, Document storage and small box storage. All our rooms vary in size and are the cheapest you'll find. We have units based in Leigh and Hindley and offer pick up of all your items. Our courier service offers same day nationwide deliveries. We have a number of vehicles depending on your needs and offers competitive prices.
